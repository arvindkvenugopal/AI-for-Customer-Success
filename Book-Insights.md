# Book Insights for Customer Success

## Never Split the Difference

### Idea

People want to be understood before they want solutions.

### Customer Success Application

When customers are frustrated, the instinct is to explain.

In many situations, listening first creates more trust than immediately presenting solutions.

### Example

During a renewal discussion, I spent more time understanding the customer's frustration than presenting action plans.

The conversation became easier once the customer felt heard.

### My Take

Many customer problems are not solved by better answers.

They're solved by better listening.
